
### UICollectionViewLayout ###

===========================================================================
DESCRIPTION:

Shows how to use a collection view to arrange views on a circle and use custom animations when inserting and removing items.
This sample app is based of Apple's presentation on UICollectionView - 'Advanced Collection Views and Building Custom Layouts' (https://developer.apple.com/videos/wwdc/2012/?id=219)
